Funding Sources for Individuals

If you are interested in joining our group but there are no open postdoctoral positions, please do consider applying for external funding. There are several sources that might be appropriate to the project and to your background.

A few examples (by no means exhaustive) are

Swiss National Science Foundation (Ambizione Program)
Marie-Curie Actions
Novartis Foundation
Human Frontier Science Program (Postdoctoral Fellowships)
