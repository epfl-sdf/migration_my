Ecole Polytechnique F√©d√©rale de Lausanne (EPFL)

EPFL is Europe’s most cosmopolitan technical university. It receives students, professors and staff from over 120 nationalities. With both a Swiss and international calling, it is therefore guided by a constant wish to open up; its missions of teaching, research and partnership impact various circles: universities and engineering schools, developing and emerging countries, secondary schools and gymnasiums, industry and economy, political circles and the general public. >> learn more

About IC

The School of Computer and Communication Sciences is one of the main European centers for education and research in the field of Information Technology.
 
With a student body comprised of more than 110 different nationalities and half of our teachers from abroad, our campus offers a dynamic and varied learning environment. We also encourage exchanges between the academic and the economic world: the Innovation Square, located nearby, is the home to numerous start-ups as well as laboratories from companies such as Logitech, Nokia and Swisscom... >> read more
