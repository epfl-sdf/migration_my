Rolex Learning Center garage

Beneath the Rolex Learning Center is an underground parking garage. The colors of the parking spaces indicate the following:

White spaces are reserved for visitors, pay at parking meter :
 
First hour CHF 1.00, then CHF 2.00 per extra hour
Free after 5 p.m. and on weekends

Green spaces are reserved for the EPFL community (staff and visitors).

Yellow spaces are reserved for Rolex Learning Center tenants.
