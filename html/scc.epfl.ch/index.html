          DSPS-SCC DSPS-SCC

          The Safety Competence Center (DSPS-SCC)  is a support aimed at protection on the workplace for researchers and students as well as for the hosts in its building. The School board decided to create this service in order to implement management as regards Occupational Safety and Health. The objectives of the DSPS-SCC aim to the daily involvement of each individual as regards SCC by setting means for prevention such as support, training and inspections as to our program MICE.
