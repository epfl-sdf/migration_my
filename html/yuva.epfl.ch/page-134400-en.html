Meet-up: swissnex India - YUVA

Join the meet-up session of YUVA members and swissnex India representatives to update each other on our activities in India and Switzerland. It will help us to know about swissnex India and also explore the possibilities to collaborate with each other. Learn more about swissnex India at their webpage.

Also, get a chance to speak directly with Dr. Balz Strasser (CEO, swissnex India) and Dr. Swetha Suresh (Manager Entrepreneurship and Innovation, swissnex India). It will be followed by a dinner.

Place and Time : Auditorium MXF1, EPFL | June 8th at 5.30 pm.

Register for the meet-up session by clicking here.
