    20.12.2013 -  Annual meeting of the Faculty of Basic Sciences (FSB) at the EPFL - "Bronze prize of safety 2013" for our laboratory

    During the annual meeting 2013 of the Faculty of Basic Sciences (FSB) at the EPFL our laboratory has been awarded the Bronze prize of safety 2013. The prize was given by the FSB and the Occupational Safety and Health Service (SB-SST) and received by the official safety delegate (COSEC) of our laboratory, Dr. Fernando Cortés Salazar. Thank you Fernando and thanks to all group members for ensuring that everyone follows the rules of safety and good lab practice!

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

    13.12.2013 - PhD defense by Encarnación (Encarni) Torralba from the University of Murcia

    With Prof. Hubert H. Girault as one of the referees, Encarni Torralba, former guest PhD student at LEPA, passed successfully her PhD defense. The thesis is entitled "Study of Ion Transfer Processes Through Liquid Membranes by Means of Voltammetrical and Chronopotentiometrical Techniques" and was submitted to the Departamento de Química Física, Facultad de Química, Universidad de Murcia, Spain. Congratulations!

    10.09.2013 - 64th Annual Meeting of the International Society of Electrochemistry in Mexico - Poster prize for Natalia Gasilova.

    Natlia Gasilova was awarded as a Poster Prize Winner at the 64th Annual Meeting of the ISE in Santiago de Querétaro, Mexico, for her poster "Droplets coupling with electrostatic-spray ionization mass spectrometry in microfluidic format". Congratulations!

    09.08.2013 - Advanced Electrochemistry Course at the National University of Colombia.

    An Advanced Electrochemistry Course was jointly organized by the National University of Colombia and LEPA, which took place in Bogota, Colombia, from 8-9 August 2013. LEPA participated with five lectures about "Fundamentals in Electrochemistry I + II", "Redox Flow Batteries", "Proteomics" and "Functionalised Soft Interfaces", given by Prof. Hubert H. Girault, and two lectures about "Scanning Electrochemical Microscopy" and "Electrochemical Read-Out of Bacterial Bioreporters", given by Dr. Fernando Cortés Salazar. The National University of Colombia participated with two lectures about "Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy" and "Simulations", both given by Prof. Marco Fidel Suarez Herrera

    Dr. Fernando Cortés Salazar and Prof. Hubert H. Girault with participants of the Advanced Electrochemistry Course in front of the Department of Chemistry of the National Unviversity of Colombia in Bogota.

    23.05.2013 - General Assembly of Electrosuisse in Baden, Switzerland - "Prix de publication technique ETG" for Veronique Amstutz and Dr. Kathryn Toghill.
