Welcome to the Laboratory of Nanobiotechnology (LNB) !

At the LNB, we work at the interface of nanotechnology and protein engineering to develop a new generation of nanobionic devices for biosensing and light-harvesting applications. Specific areas of interest include:

  • Nanotube photophysics
  • Bio-hybrid optoelectronics
  • Single-molecule fluorescence sensing
  • Directed evolution
  • Biological photovoltaics (PVs)
