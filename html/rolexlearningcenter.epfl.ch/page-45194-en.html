EPFL Alumni

The EPFL Alumni

The EPFL Alumni service, with over 29'000 alumni spread over 80 countries, is an exclusive network of professionals working in research, management, industry and public service. Members have access to an invaluable community of people connected to the EPFL that can provide career and project advice. With the online directory, privileged contacts, continuing education and access to innovative events on the campus and abroad, members multiply their networking opportunities among a vast pool of highly qualified individuals.
