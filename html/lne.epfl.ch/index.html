LNE LNE
Our laboratory is a multidisciplinary environment promoting cross-fertilization among various expertise. We bring materials science, engineering, life science, and medicine together by the convergence of physicists, engineers, neuroscientists, and ophthalmologists cooperating to accomplish innovative projects. Our mission is the implementation of neuroprostheses for fighting blindness. Ultimately, we aim at translating our research findings into clinical practice.
