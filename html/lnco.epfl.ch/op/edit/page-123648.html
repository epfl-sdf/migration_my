Human Enhancement and Neurotechnology

The Laboratory of Cognitive Neuroscience has introduced novel approaches to the neuroscientific study of self-consciousness, by combining cognitive neuroscience methods with engineering techniques such as robotics, haptics, virtual reality. Current research in the lab focuses in developing technological approaches to study and potentially boost human cognitive functions and experience.
