PIXE PIXE

ENAC Interdisciplinary Platform for X-ray micro-CT

The X-ray micro-CT PIXE platform was created in 2016 at the Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) by five laboratories of the ENAC School (Architectural, Civil and Environmental Engineering).

 

 

The PIXE platform is designed to perform very high quality radiographic and tomographic imaging on a wide range of natural or engineered materials. It will provide researchers and students with quantitative and non destructive data on the density and structure of their studied materials.

This micro CT system has no equivalent in Switzerland. Due to its dimensional stability, its spatial resolution, its capacity to accept very heavy samples and its design, it is especially well adapted for engineering purposes.

This platform has a wide range of possible applications in various domains. It has consequently also the objective to develop interdisciplinary research and scientific collaborations,

For further information, please contact: pixe@epfl.ch
