Open Positions

PhD students

Candidates should apply online to the EDMS doctoral program, and can check on the site for the availability of PhD fellowships in our laboratory.

 

Student projects

Our laboratory is hosting motivated EPFL students for semester, bachelor and master projects. The projects are linked to our ongoing research work on telomeres, involving molecular biological, biochemical and cell biological techniques and human cell culture.

 

Summer research program

Our lab accepts one student every summer from our International Summer Research Program.
