Secondary navigation

The BioEM Facility is part of the Life Science Faculty and affiliated with the Centre Interdisciplinaire de Microscopie Electronique (cime.epfl.ch). The laboratory is equipped for preparing and imaging biological samples of many types . For any enquiries about sample preparation, imaging, and training please contact staff in the facility or in the CIME.
