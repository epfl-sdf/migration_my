      Pole of Excellence at the foot of the peaks

      Principally dedicated to scientific research in the fields of energy, health and the environment, the EPFL Valais Wallis campus currently brings together 160 people in Sion the heart of the Valais, with the aim to reach 400 in the coming years.
      4 faculties and a college are involved in the development of the campus : the School of Basic Sciences (SB) and its Institute of Chemical Sciences and Engineering (ISIC), the School of Life Sciences (SV) and its Center for Neuroprosthetics (CNP), School of Engineering (STI), the School of Architecture, Civil and Environmental Engineering (ENAC) as well as the College of Management of Technology (CDM).
