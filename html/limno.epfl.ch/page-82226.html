Facilities

LIMNO has developed expertise in both the synthesis of inorganic, nanoparticulate, and organic semiconductor materials. We have the ability to process these materials into thin films and examine their optoelectronic and electrochemical performance in various device architectures. In addition, we have the ability to characterize thin film morphology on the nanometer length scale. Below is a gallery of the main tools present in our laboratories.
