            Speakers

            Workshop speakers

            Some 10 speakers will give presentations on the various workshop topics. The speakers are experts in their respective fields with either an academic, consulting, public service, or industry background. In keeping with the workshop's focus on the MENA region, the majority of speakers will be MENA-based. International experts will provide insights into industrial best practices and technologies less common in MENA.

            The speaker list is regularly updated as we receive the confirmations from the speakers..
