        Getting here

        Coming by plane and by train
        Genève-Cointrin is the nearest airport (40 minutes). The train from Zürich Airport takes approximately
        2h30 to Lausanne. From these airports, you can take a train to Lausanne Gare, then
        the metro (Lausanne Gare - Metro M2> Lausanne Flon > Metro M1 - EPFL).
        Alternatively, you can take a train to Renens VD , then the metro (Renens VD - Metro M1> EPFL).
         
        Coming by car
        The coordinates N46.52184, E6.56488 will take you directly to the parking lot at the campus.

        Read More about LMS

        The Soil Mechanics Laboratory gives priority to the protection from geo-hazards and industrial damage to the environment, landforms and structures. Our experimental and modelling ressources are mobilised to understand, describe and predict the environmental impact of the technologies of future days.
