Teaching

The Laboratory for Quantum Magnetism provide practical research oriented training for:

  • Interns and summer students
  • Master students (Travaux Pratiques IV)
  • Specialization
  • PhD students

The head of laboratory, Prof. Henrik M. Ronnow teaches several courses from fundamental physics to advanced solid state physics plus a number of guest lectures and summer schools. The laboratory members all provide teaching services in form of tutoring, mentoring, course administration etc.

Teaching 2016/2017

Projects

We constantly offer very interesting projects on quantum magnetism to motivated students. LQM also gladly provides contact to student exercises at Laboratory for Neutron Scattering at the Paul Scherrer Institute (PSI) in Villigen.
