    • Find an activity in the region – although the hike was nice, we have to be prepared for bad weather.

     

    Feedback on the Project Management (Galina, Antonio)

     

    The course had very good feedback from participants. The 2-day course from now on will cost CHF 5000 (without the discount from the first time), plus train and hotel for the trainer. Since it was well attended and many were in the waiting list, we discussed the possibility of doing it twice a year. The STS proposed to do 4 times a year, we decided to try 3. Galina asked for CHF 3000 from STS for the next ones. The STS will decide on the budget for next year in the net weeks and will come back to us to discuss the possibilities.

     

    Feedback on the Funding resources for postdocs (Pamela)

     

    It was mainly focused on opportunities for early postdocs. We decided that for the next time we will ask the speaker to, for at least for half of the time, discuss more about funding opportunities for late postdocs that would be interested in group leader positions in Switzerland and Europe.

     

    Novartis visit 2017 (Marianna)

     

    Everything is set, Marianna will go over the submitted abstracts this week.

     

    Postdoc welcome event (Petra – not present)

     

    Both Petra and Zerrin can’t make it on the 4th of December, so we will have to postpone.

     

    General Assembly/committee elections 2017 (Pamela, Antonio)

     

    It will happen on the 4th December. Marianna is willing to be the next president, Jamshid the next VP, and Galina will be the next treasurer.

     

    Other issues
