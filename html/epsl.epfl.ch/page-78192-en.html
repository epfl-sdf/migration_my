Laser heating

Double-sided fiber laser heating system

Our laser heating system is used to generate temperatures of Earth's deep interior inside the diamond anvil cell. The 200 W infrared laser beam is split by polarization to independently heat the two sides of the sample. Heating is monitored visually and by spectroscopy of the blackbody radiation from the sample.

Contact: Richard Gaal
