Meeting Bubbles

At the library, five transparent bubbles provide cover for ten meeting rooms. These soundproof spaces are ideal for group workshops.
Reservation of bubbles, managed by the reception of the EPFL, is made only by mail Monday to Friday from 10h00 to 16h30: learningcenter@epfl.ch. (at least 2h00 before)

Please denote: Date, Time, Nb of persons, Name and Sciper

Booking possible 3 days before and for 3 hours maximum

After booking hours bubbles are in free access with code 0000
Meetings bubbles are exclusively reserved for students EPFL / UNIL and EPFL employees and for 2 persons minimum.
