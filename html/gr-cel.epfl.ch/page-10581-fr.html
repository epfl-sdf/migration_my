Services & Equipements

Services

Our offer for analytical services can be summarized as follow :

Soil parameters: water content, pH and electrical conductivity, exchange capacity (cationic), density, particle size distribution (sedimentation and laser), porosity, retention curves, hydraulic conductivity and others under request.

Anions & cations (ion chromatography)

Elemental analysis by ICP-MS and ICP-OES

Analysis of organic micro pollutants, e.g. POPs, pesticides, PCBs, PAHs, pharmaceuticals, etc... (others under request), in different matrices. Measures by GC and HPLC with specific detectors or coupling with mass-spectrometry.
