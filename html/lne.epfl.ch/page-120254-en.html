SPECIFIC OPENINGS

We do not have any specific openings. 

spontaneous applications

Even if we may not have any specific openings, we welcome spontaneous applications from excellent and highly motivated students. If you are interested in an interdisciplinary research program, please feel free to contact us! 

Applications should include a detailed CV and a link to 3-min long video answering the following questions:

- Why you would like to do research in Switzerland and at EPFL?
- What attracted you to our laboratory?
- How your background and previous experience will enrich our laboratory?
- What do you expect to receive from your experience in our laboratory?

 

UNDERGRADUATE STUDENTS

We are always interested in motivated students willing to learn and develop skills in micro-fabrication, neurotechnology, and neuroscience. We prefer to host students for intensive periods instead of spreading the project over the semester. Therefore, if you wish to perform your project during the winter or summer break, you are welcome! 

EPFL bachelor and master students interested in a laboratory project (Bachelor, Semester, or Master) can contact us to know more about our ongoing research activities. Projects are dynamically changing every semester and we offer projects to students from Bioengineering, Materials Science, Microengineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Computer Science. Please specify what type of research attract you most, we can customize the projects according to your learning interest.

Non-EPFL students are welcome to apply for extended research stay only (> 5 months).
