        GHGT 13

        The Laboratory for Soil Mechanics – Chair Gaz Naturel – Petrosvibri participated and co-organized the 13th International conference on Greenhouse Gas Control Technologies. The event took place at the SwissTech Convention Center in Lausanne (Switzerland) on the 14 – 18 November 2016.

         
        Openig session. (Photo by Marino Trevisani | marino.ch)
         
        A huge number of scientists, researchers, professionals (around 1000) coming from Universities, Institutions and Company from all over the world, made presentations focusing on the technologies and the developments in the field of CO2 Capture and Storage (CCS). 
         
        During the conference, an exhibitor space has been reserved for the Chair Gaz Naturel – Petrosvibri (EPFL), in which were presented the research activities done at the Chair, focusing on the advanced multiphysical testing and modelling of CO2 storage.
         The CO2 group of the Chair Gaz Naturel. (Photo by Marino Trevisani | marino.ch)
         
        Dr. Chao Li presented a poster intitled “Caprock and surface deformation induced by carbon dioxide injection” in the Geomechanics poster session.
        Dr. Roman Makhnenko presented a paper intitled “Hydromechanical aspects of CO2 breakthrough into clay-rich caprock” in the session Geomechanics (1).
        The participants at the conference had the opportunity to visit the Laboratory for Soil Mechanics – Chair Gaz Naturel – Petrosvibri more details at the following link http://lms.epfl.ch/page-139187-fr.html).

        En savoir plus sur LMS

        Le Laboratoire de Mécanique des Sols donne la priorité à la protection contre les risques géologiques et les dommages à l'environnement industriel, les reliefs et les structures. Nos ressources expérimentales et de modélisation sont mobilisées pour comprendre, décrire et prévoir l'impact environnemental des technologies du futur.
