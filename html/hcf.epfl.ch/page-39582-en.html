Antibodies

Fluorescent and enzymatic secondary antibodies are available at the core facility AI0342 and a supply is available at the BIOP for people at EPFL. The fluorescent secondary antibodies are provided by Molecular Probes (Invitrogen), the other ones by Jackson Immunologicals Research.

Fees will be sent on demand, please write an email to: info.hcf@epfl.ch
