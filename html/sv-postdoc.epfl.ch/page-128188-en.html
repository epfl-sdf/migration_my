    Meeting with Gisou:

     

    Nic summarized the meeting she, Pame and Antonio had with Gisou:

     

    1)     Teaching: Postdocs have the possibility to teach and even propose a course, not for undergraduates, but for graduate students. Nic and Antonio will contact the coordinators of the Doctoral Programs (EDMS – Daniel Constan/Nicola Harris; EDBB - Mateo del Peraro; EDNE – Michael Herzog) to see if they agree in spreading the word among postdocs. She suggested that we could also have a list of postdocs willing to help, and in the end it was decided that this would work in different levels: first the person try to discuss with a postdoc (from a list we will prepare), if necessary we would put them in contact with a professor from the list.
