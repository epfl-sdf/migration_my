                                              Agreement for the joint EPFL and UCL PhD

                                              On January 19th 2016, an agreement for the joint EPFL and Université catholique de Louvain (UCL) PhD has been signed, in order to establish and develop academic and scientific cooperation by promoting the mobility of PhD candidates. Joint doctoral candidates share their thesis work time between the two institutions and are awarded a single joint degree from both of them.

                                              Before to hire a candidate for an EPFL - UCL joint PhD, a specific agreement has to be agreed upon. Please contact the EPFL doctoral school gestion.edoc@epfl.ch when anticipating such collaboration.
