Minors

Minors for Master students in Computer science

In order to pursue a particular aspect of your studies or to specialize in conjunction with another section at EPFL, as a student in Computer science, you can choose a secondary field of study and earn a “minor” degree, in addition to your master’s studies.

A minor curriculum corresponds to 30 credits that can be earned among a selection of courses proposed in one of the other sections at EPFL. These 30 credits will be added to the 90 credits of the master’s study plan in Computer science, according to the regulations of the section.


The following list provides the recommended minors proposed to the Computer science students:

a)
  • Biocomputing (IN)
  • Biomedical technologies (MT)
  • Computational Science and Engineering (MA)
  • Spatial technologies (EL)
  • Technology Management and Entrepreneurship (MTE)  
b)        
It is possible to prepare other minor degrees: see http://sac.epfl.ch/study_plans (see under the link for “Minors with specific courses”)
Rules
  • The choice of a minor must be approved of by the Deputy head of the section (Sylviane Dal Mas).
  • The choice of a minor must be approved of by the ‘minor director’.
  • The minor degree is considered achieved when at least 30 ECTSs are earned from the approved courses.
  • The student must acquire these 30 minor ECTSs before beginning a master’s project.
  • Some minors (for example Financial Engineering) accept a limited number of students.

Procedure

The first week of the first semester, you must contact the ‘minor director’ of your choice, in order to validate your list of chosen courses. With your director’s approval, you then must contact the Deputy head of the section (Mme Sylviane Dal Mas), in order to organize your minor study plan.
The registration form (pdf) must be filled out with the chosen courses. This form must be signed by the director of the specific minor and then it must be given to the administrative office of the Computer Science Section.
Deadline for registration with IS-Academia: end of the 1st semester of the Master studies
Do not sign up for a minor at the beginning of the autumn semester. You have one semester to decide. But you can go ahead and sign up for your minor courses as of the first semester.

Minors offered by the Computer science section to other students of EPFL

The Computer Science Section offers the following minor degrees to students from other sections at EPFL:

  • Computer science (S. Dal Mas)
  • Biocomputing (M. Salathé)
  • Computer engineering (P. Ienne)
 
Admissions
For all EPFL students: http://sac.epfl.ch/study_plans(see the link – at the bottom of the webpage - for ‘Minors with specific lectures’). Approval from your section is required before registration.
Procedure
The first week of the first semester, you must contact the Deputy head of the Computer Science Section (Mme Sylviane Dal Mas) to organize your minor study plan.
The registration form (pdf) must be filled out with the chosen courses. This form must be signed by the director of the specific minor: One copy must be given to the administrative office of the Computer Science Section and one copy must be given to the administrative office of your own section.
Deadline for Registration with IS-Academia: end of the 1st semester of the Master studies

Note: The list of courses for each minor can be found on the website of Registar’s office (SAC), at the bottom of the page “Study plans & Rules”.
