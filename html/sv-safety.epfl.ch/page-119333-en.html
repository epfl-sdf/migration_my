Safety glasses

Eye protection is absolutely required when working with chemicals or when there is a risk of projection of solids or liquids or when protection is needed against dangerous wavelengths such as UV.

Normal safety glasses can be obtained free at the SV Faculty store

For medical safety glasses, follow the procedure explained on the form "Order form for medical safety glasses" on this website.
