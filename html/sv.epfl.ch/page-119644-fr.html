EPFL Life Science Seminar series

This new Life Science wide seminar series has been launched in October 2009 that we hope will bring together people from all institutes. Speakers have been chosen based on the excellence of their science, the general interest of their research topics and their ability to communicate with a broad interest audience. So everyone should understand any talk as remote it might seem from the field they work in.

They will be held every Friday at 11:00 in SV1717.1, followed by a small apero to stimulate discussions and interactions within and outside of the EPFL School of Life Sciences.
