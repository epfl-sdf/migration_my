Presentation of Baramine

BARAMINE 2016-2017

Baramine... a name, a symbol engraved in every student's memory!


Baramine is a bar organised from the students in chemistry to entertain the crowd during the evenements organised at EPFL.
The collected funds are used to finance the study trip. 

More informations are found on Baramine website
