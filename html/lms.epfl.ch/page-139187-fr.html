        GHGT13: LMS Lab visit, 2016

         More than 90 participants of the GHGT-13 conference visited the Laboratory of Soil Mechanics at EPFL. This event took place in the framework of the “13th International Conference on Greenhouse Gas Control Technologies” (SwissTech Convention Center - Lausanne, 14-18 November 2016).
         
         
        People from a number of International Universities, Companies and Industries participated to the visit asking questions and sharing opinions with the LMS personnel who showed the laboratory equipment and the ongoing activities.
        Here are some details of the visit:
         

        Prof. Alessio Ferrari gave an overview of the laboratory activities and some details about the equipment and the materials tested.

        Mrs. Valentina Favero showed the ongoing about nuclear waste storage.

        Mr. Alberto Minardi explained the gas shales related laboratory tests.

        Prof. Roman Makhnenko showed the activities related to CO2 storage.

        En savoir plus sur LMS

        Le Laboratoire de Mécanique des Sols donne la priorité à la protection contre les risques géologiques et les dommages à l'environnement industriel, les reliefs et les structures. Nos ressources expérimentales et de modélisation sont mobilisées pour comprendre, décrire et prévoir l'impact environnemental des technologies du futur.
