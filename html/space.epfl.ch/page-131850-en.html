          EPFL International Space Summer Camp

           

          You are a Space Enthusiast?

          You would like to know more about the Russian, Swiss or Chinese Space Programmes?
          You always dreamed to meet cosmonauts, astronauts or taikonauts and visit space facilities?

          Then you should attend one of the three joint Space Summer camps available at the Swiss Space Center in cooperation with Bauman Moscow State Technical University (BMSTU), Russia and Beihang University, Beijing, China

          Who can participate?

          Any students enrolled at EPFL, ETHZ , Swiss Universities or Universities of Applied Sciences in their last year of Bachelor's degree, in Master's or PhD degrees are elligible to apply.

          Specialisation in aerospace is welcomed but not mandatory.

          Programme

          The programme, as described belove, will include

          • Tour of space facilities in  Switzerland
          • Meeting with professionals working on the front edge of space science and technology
          • Lectures on various subjects related to space exploration
          • Scientific workshops
          • Cultural programme

           

          23 August 2015: Arrival of participants

          24 August 2015:

          • *09:00 - 11:30 - BC410:  Welcome & introduction, EPFL & Swiss Space Center presentations, BMSTU & Beihang Universities presentations
          • 11:30 - 12:30 - BC410: Team Work Introduction
          • *12:30 - 13:30: Lunch
          • 13:30 - 17:00: Workshop Introductions & lectures
          • *19:00 - 21:30: Regional Evening: each participant should bring beverage or food from his/her region to share with other participants

          25 August 2015:

          • 09:00-12:30: Workshops (in different stations)
          • *12:30 - 13:30: Lunch
          • 13:30 - 17:15: Workshops (in different stations)
          • *17:15 - 19:00 - BC 420: Lecture by Prof. Claude Nicollier
          • *19:00 - 21:30: Summer Camp Dinner

          26 August 2015: 

          • 07:20: departure for Ste-Croix
          • 09:00 - 12:00: Workshops (in different stations)
          • 12:00 - 13:30: Lunch
          • *13:30 - 16:00: Visit of CIMA Museum
          • *16:00 - 19:00: Hike from Ste-Croix to les Cluds
          • *19:00 - 21:30: Dinner in Alpage with activities organised by students
          • 21:30: Return to EPFL

          27 August 2015:

          • 09:00 - 12:30: Workshops (in different stations)
          • *12:30 - 13:30: Lunch
          • 13:30 - 17:15: Workshops (in different stations)
          • *19:00 - 21:30: Barbecue at EPFL with activities organised by students

          28 August 2015:

          • *08:30 - 12:00 - BC 410: Final Presentations
          • *12:00 - 12:30 - BC 410: Certificate Ceremony
          • *12:30 - 13:30: Lunch
          • *13:30 - 14:30 - BC 410: Conclusion of the Summer Camp
          • *19:00 - 21:30: "Cheese" Night  (fondue or raclette and other Swiss specialties)

          29 August 2015:

          • *All day: hike or shopping or museums in Lausanne

          30 August 2015: Departure of participants

           

          Lunches and coffee breaks will be organised at EPFL.

          If you wish to participate in one or several activities starting with *, please contact Lise-Loup Antoniadis for detailed information (conditions, location, etc). Registration is mandatory!

           

           

           

          • Programme_EPFL_August 2015_shortV.pdf

          How to Apply?

          Please fill in the online form and prepare a motivation letter explaining why you would like to apply to this summer school.

          Please note that you are also elligible to apply to Beijing OR Moscow Summer camps.

           

          (Students who are not enrolled in Swiss Universities shall upload the scan of their passport identification page)

          Deadline for applications: 30 April 2015
