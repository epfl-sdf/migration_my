          2007 2007

          2007

          13.12.07 The students of the EPFL Physics Section will have the possibility to select the Minor in Space Technologies starting with the academic year 2008-2009.
          12.12.07 ESA currently has a call open for experiments on board sounding rockets and stratospheric balloons to be launched from Kiruna in Sweden. The programmes are called Rocket/Balloon Experiments for University Students and the deadline to send a letter of intention is set to 7 January 2008.
          04.12.07 Renewal of the cooperation agreement between Oerlikon Space and the Space Center EPFL for the duration 2008-2010.
          03.12.07 The European Space Agency has published the list of Young Graduates Traineeships (YGT) which allows fresh young engineers to spend one year at ESA. Deadline to apply for most position is set to 14 December 2007. More information available at http://www.esa.int/hr/ygt.htm.
          18.10.07 A training course of the ECSS standards will be organised at EPFL on 27-28 November 2007. More information ... The deadline to register is set to 10 November 2007.
          05.10.07 The presentations made during the 3rd EPFL Space Research Day on 4 October 2007 may be browsed in PDF format.
          07.09.07 To celebrate the launch of the first man-made satellite 50 years ago, the Space Center EPFL will organize the 3rd EPFL Space Research Day on the topic: "Early days of Space conquest and visions for the future". The event will take on Thursday 4 October 2007 from 13h30 onwards at the EPFL Polydôme, exactly 50 years day for day after the launch of Sputnik.
          The workshop will be followed by the 2nd EPFL Innovation day at 18h00 on the same day also dedicated to this celebration.
          01.09.07 A presentation about the Minor in Space Technologies will take place on Wednesday 19 September in auditorium ELA-1 at EPFL from 12h15 to 13h15(PDF file of the presentation). It will also include a short presentation on the EPFL SwissCube satellite.
          26.06.07 Interested in developing a suborbital spaceplane project coordinated by the ACE (Astronaute Club Européen)? The Student Aerospace Challenge is looking for undergraduate or graduate students who are currently studying in Europe. More information can be obtained from Mrs Pénélope Leyland at EPFL or by visiting the Student Aerospace Challenge web page.
          20.06.07 Interested by a trip to Mars? ESA is looking for 12 volunteers who are ready to participate in the preparations for a mission to Mars including a 520-day simulated mission to Mars in 2008/2009.
          14.06.07 Dr. Butler Hine, NASA-Ames, will give a seminar about Small Spacecraft Development at NASA Ames Research Center on 21 June 2007 at 17h in Auditorium CM1 at EPFL. The entrance is free and the abstract of his presentation is on-line.
          28.05.07 The list of semester and Master projects offered by the Space Center EPFL for the Fall 2007 is now on-line.
          05.04.07 Based on the recently-approved EPFL strategic initiative, a call for PhD research in the space domain and ESA Networking/Partnering Initiative is now open. The deadline to submit proposals has been set to 31 May 2007
          28.03.07 Nomination of Claude Nicollier as « professeur ordinaire » at EPFL.
          26.03.07 If you are interested in the applications of satellite data for Earth observation, then please attend the ENVISAT-2007 symposium which will be held in Montreux from 23 to 27 April 2007.
          01.03.07 The "Haute Ecole Valaisanne" has become the third academic member of the Space Center EPFL.
          16.02.07 The Space Center EPFL is able to sponsor "seed-money" research activities involving EPFL labs and other partners. More ...
          29.01.07 The Alpbach Summer School is open to 60 selected young science and/or engineering graduates from among the member and cooperating states of the European Space Agency (ESA) and will be dedicated this year to the theme "Astrobiology: Life Detection in and from Space". More information... (application deadline set to 31 March 2007)
          22.01.07 Chance for an European student to join the NASA 2007 summer academy. Deadline to send the applications set to 18 February 2007. More details here.
          10.01.07 The SwissCube Satellite Project will hold a presentation of the status of the project and of the proposed summer semester and master projects on January 17, 12h-13h00 at the Polydome.
