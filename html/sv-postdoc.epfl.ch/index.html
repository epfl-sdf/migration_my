    Dear Life Sciences Postdoctoral fellows,

    Welcome to SV Postdoc Association, an association by the postdocs for the postdocs and open to all postdocs from all over the EPFL campus.

    The main goals of the association are networking, organizing career events, inform you about interesting events and be a gateway for prospective postdoc candidates. Check out the services we offer to our members and join us (also on LinkedIn)! Coming events below, looking forward to meeting you!
