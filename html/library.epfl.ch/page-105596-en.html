                                          Opening hours and access

                                          Special summer schedules

                                          From July 9 to August 19, the Library is open with reduced services. Registrations and withdrawals of documents orderd from the NEBIS network will only be possible from 12:00 to 18:00. If you have any question, feel free to ask our assistants. They can answer directly or forward to librarians.

                                          Hours

                                          The Library is open every day from Monday to Sunday from 7:00 to 24:00  (excluding 1st August and 25th December).

                                          The librarians provide services to the public from Monday to Friday from 8:00 to 20:00 :

                                          • help and support
                                          • registration
                                          • borrowing ordered / reserved documents
                                          • borrowing CDs and DVDs

                                          Anybody who is registered at the Library, or in NEBIS, may borrow documents at any time by using the self-service loan stations (self-check). Documents  can be returned in returns boxes available in the Library.

                                          Online collections (periodicals, ebooks and databases) are accessible remotely for EPFL members using VPN. External users can access these resources at the Library.

                                          The Rolex Learning Center and the Library are accessible to limited mobility persons.

                                          Audio tour in the Library

                                          Are you a new comer or visiting the EPFL? Would you like to know more about the main services and collections of the Library? It is now possible to carry out an autonomous visit of the Library in audio-guided form, directly from your smartphone or tablet!

                                          Follow the circuit of the eleven colorful flags on your own, by listening to the audio-guide commentary accessible through QR-codes distributed throughout the Library. Meet us at the welcome desk to start the tour and borrow headphones and a tablet.
