Welcome to SMaL!

We are part of the Institute of Materials (IMX) at EPFL and conduct fundamental as well as applied research in the field of soft matter. We employ drops to control the local composition and structure of materials and relate these parameters to their properties. For example, we use of airborne drops to produce composite particles with unusual structures and emulsion drops to fabricate structured hydrogels with locally varying compositions.
