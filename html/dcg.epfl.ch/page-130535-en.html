Grigory Ivanov

   I am a postdoc in the Discrete and Computational Geometry Group of János Pach at EPFL. I did my Phd at MIPT in Moscow under the supervision of Grigorii Ivanov. My research interests include some topics of combinatorics and convex geometry, geometry of Banach spaces.                                                      

 

 

e-mail:  grimivanov@gmail.com, grigory,ivanov@epfl.ch
